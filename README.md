# Vibrascope Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Vibrascope does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

The app uses your device's built-in accelerometer sensor to measure 
vibrations locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.

Ads are served by Google AdMob. Google may collect device identifiers 
for ad personalization in accordance with their own privacy policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

For questions contact: honkingwheat@gmail.com
